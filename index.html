Discover New People

TEXTS

Simple, Reliable Messaging

Message your friends and family for free*. Mengen uses your phone's Internet connection to send messages so you can avoid SMS fees.

VOICE AND VIDEO CALLS

Speak Freely

With voice calls, you can talk to your friends and family for free*, even if they're in another country. And with free* video calls , you can have face-to-face conversations for when voice or text just isn't enough.

END-TO-END ENCRYPTION

Security by Default

When end-to-end encrypted, your messages and calls are secured so only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to them, and nobody in between, not even Mengen.

GROUP CHAT

Groups to keep in touch

Keep in touch with the groups of people that matter the most, like your family
or coworkers. With group chats, you can share messages, photos, and videos with up to 10 people at once
. You can also name your group, mute or customize notifications, and more.

MENGEN ON WEB AND DESKTOP

Keep the Conversation Going

With Mengen on the web and desktop, you can seamlessly sync all of your
chats to your computer so that you can chat on whatever device is most convenient for you.
Download the desktop app or visit web.mengen.com to get started

PHOTOS AND VIDEOS

Share Moments that Matter

Send photos and videos on Mengen instantly. You can even capture the moments
that matter to you most with a built-in camera.
With Mengen, photos and videos send quickly even if you're on a slow connection.

VOICE MESSAGES

Say What's On Your Mind

Sometimes, your voice says it all. With just one tap you can record a Voice Message, perfect for a quick hello or a longer story.

VIDEO CALLS

Video calls use your phone's Internet connection, instead of your cell plan's voice minutes, so you don't have to worry about expensive calling charges.
* Data charges may apply. Contact your provider for details.
